About Me

Hello, my name is Augustine Opoku-Kesse from Ghana. I am an Applied Mathematician who works as a researcher, data analyst, and software engineer at Werld Analytics, a research and data solutions company in based in Kumasi, Ghana.

I am currently training as a FullStack Engineer in the Amalitech Graduate Trainee Program. My aim is to combine this skill with my knowledge in Applied Mathematics to create indigenous Technologies and provide digital solutions for all sectors of the Ghanaian Economy.

In my free time, I research and read about future innovations in computer technology, and developments in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. I am motivated by people who challenge the status quo and rise up against the odds.
